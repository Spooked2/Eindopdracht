★ ★ ★ ☆ ☆

The Shocker, also known as Montana, is one of the lower ranking goons. He relies on some sort of shocking glove thingy, a pretty fancy piece of tech, hence his name. While he's still quite a threat, I've gotten his moves down enough to where I can reliably take him down when he's alone. It's a different story when he's working with some other goons however.
